# Project Shadowfall

**Pineapple-Powered Alien Tech for LilyGo Devices**

Welcome to **Project Shadowfall** – a wild collection of custom watch the pineapple-loving alien who crash-landed on Earth.

After his saucer smashed into a rual field, he decided humanity needed a serious upgrade: vibrant visuals, futuristic interfaces, and a healthy dose of tropical absurdity. These themes blend glowing alien tech with juicy pineapple aesthetics – because nothing says “advanced civilization” like a radioactive pineapple waving at you from your wrist.

### Supported Sizes / Devices
I’ve created **six** different resolutions so Bruce can crash-land on pretty much any LilyGo hardware:

- **AMOLED Square** – 466 × 466
- **AMOLED Tall** – 240 × 536
- **Long Version** – 640 × 180
- **T-DEC3** – 320 × 240
- **T-Display** – 240 × 135
- **T-Display S3 & T-Embedded** – 320 × 170

Each size lives in its own top-level folder at the root of the repo / SD card.

### Installation Instructions

1. Copy **all the main folders** straight to the root of your LilyGo’s SD card.
(No extra nesting – just drop the six size folders right there.)

2. **Boot Animation / Boot Wave Files (IMPORTANT)**
Inside **one** of the theme folders you’ll find a subfolder called `boot stuff`.
It contains the boot animation file and the boot wave file.
**Do NOT leave them in the subfolder.**

→ Take those two files out and place them **directly on the root** of the SD card (the very front / top level, next to the theme folders).
The firmware needs to see them at the root level to load them properly on startup.

3. Insert the SD card, power on your LilyGo, and watch Bruce harvest your ignorance in glorious high-res pineapple glory.
